Object of the Game
Buy hideouts as you travel the Outer Rim and 
confront Imperial enemies who seek to obtain 
Grogu! Earn Imperial credits by winning 
battles against those enemies and 
by charging other players rent. The game 
ends when the final enemy, Moff Gideon,  
has been defeated. Then the player with 
the most Imperial credits wins! 
Be vigilant! The game also ends if any 
Imperial enemy obtains Grogu. 
Then all players lose!
