Aim of the game
Buy hideouts as you travel around an increasingly dangerous galaxy in which Imperial enemies threaten the safety of The Child! 
Earn Imperial credits by winning battles against those enemies and by charging other players rent. The game ends when the 
final enemy, Moff Gideon, has been defeated. The player with the most Imperial credits wins!  
Be vigilant! The game also ends if any Imperial enemy obtains The Child. Then all players lose!
