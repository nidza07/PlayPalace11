TOP TIPS
To keep your game short and sweet, don’t use house rules!
Never loan credits to other players or make deals not to charge each other payments. 
Never put credits in the center of the board; you don’t get a bonus for landing on Free Parking! 
JEDI 
STARFIGHTERS
As soon as you get a color set, you can start adding Jedi 
Starfighters (you don’t have to wait for your turn). 
To add a Jedi Starfighter, pay the Bank the cost on the alliance 
card, and place a Jedi Starfighter on that alliance. You must add 
evenly. You cannot add a second Jedi Starfighter to an alliance until 
you’ve added one to each alliance in the set. You can add up to 5 
Jedi Starfighters to an alliance.  
One Jedi Starfighter left? Auction it! 
If multiple players want to buy the last Jedi Starfighter, the Banker 
must auction it. Bids start at 10 and anyone can increase the bid 
by as little as 10. You don’t need to follow turn order. Payment 
goes to the Bank.

(whether he is with another player’s token or not), and place him at your side. Now you may use 
your character power, described below and on your character card.

DEALS & TRADES
You can trade alliances with other players at any time—but you 
may never sell or trade Yoda.
You must sell all Jedi Starfighters on a color set to the Bank before 
you can give an alliance back to the Bank or trade an alliance to 
another player. You cannot sell or trade Jedi Starfighters to another 
player.
Alliances can be traded for other alliances and/or The Force cards 
and Get Out of Jail Free cards. The amount is decided by the players 
making the deal.
No Jedi Starfighters left?  
You can’t buy any until 
someone sells theirs back 
to the Bank.

HELP! I CAN’T PAY
1.  Try to raise credits.
If you owe payment, a The Force or The Dark Side card fee, or 
you land on a heroic figure that no player has formed an alliance 
with and can’t pay, try to raise credits by selling Jedi Starfighters 
back to the Bank, or by selling The Force cards and/or Get Out of 
Jail Free cards to other players for an agreed-upon price.
SELLING JEDI STARFIGHTERS
Sell Jedi Starfighters to the Bank for their cost. Jedi 
Starfighters must be sold evenly across the color set.
Still owe credits? 
If you still owe a player payment and don’t have any Jedi 
Starfighters to sell, you must transfer one of your alliances to that 
player to settle your debt. If you still owe the Bank, return one 
alliance to the Bank to settle your debt.  
2.  IF YOU DON’T HAVE ANY ALLIANCES 
LEFT, YOU ARE BANKRUPT AND OUT 
OF THE GAME!
Do you owe another player?  
Give them any The Force cards and/or Get Out of Jail Free cards 
you have. 
If Yoda is at your side, place  
him on the GO space.
Do you owe the Bank? 
Return any The Force cards and/or Get Out of Jail Free cards you 
have to the bottom of the appropriate deck. 
If Yoda is at your side, place  
him on the GO space.
The remaining players keep playing until  
there is only one person left in the game.  
That player is the winner—and brings  
balance to the Force!
Note: If that player ends up on the In Jail space, 
do not collect any credits from them—a Jedi always 
chooses compassion, never greed. ™

How to win
Move around the board forming alliances with as many heroic 
figures as you can. The more alliances you form, the more 
payments you’ll be able to collect from other players. If you’re 
the last player with credits when all other players have lost 
theirs, you win!
Who goes First?
Each player rolls the die.  
The highest roller starts, and play moves to the left.
On your turn
1. Roll the die.
2.  Move your token (and Yoda if you have him) clockwise  
that number of spaces.
3.   Where did you land? Carry out the rules of that board 
space. See THE BOARD SPACES. 
Did you pass 
or land on GO? 
Immediately take 
Yoda and attach 
your token 
to his base!  
See YODA. 
4. Your turn ends. Pass the die to your left.
Start playing! 
That’s all you need to know, so get going.  
Look up the spaces as you land on them.
Form alliances with heroes from across the galaxy!  
Properties are alliances with heroic figures. When you land on a heroic figure, you must form 
an alliance with them. If you form alliances with both heroic figures in a color set, you can 
strengthen those alliances by adding Jedi Starfighters to those spaces, and collect more 
payments from other players!
Get help from Master Yoda!  
Each character token has a unique power. You can 
use your character’s power whenever you have Yoda 
at your side. When you pass or land on the GO space, 
take Yoda (in addition to collecting credits), and place 
him with your token. Then you can use the power 
on your character card!
Jump to hyperspace! 
When you land on a Ship space, you may jump to 
hyperspace, which means you can move to any 
heroic figure between that Ship and the next one on 
the board! If you pass GO, you do not collect credits 
or place Yoda at your side as you normally would. 
Use the power of The Force cards! 
When you land on one of The Force spaces,  
draw a Force card. These cards might allow  
you to place Yoda at your side immediately,  
collect additional credits, or cancel a  
Dark Side card played against you!

ACTION SPACES
Just Visiting
Don’t worry—you’re Just Visiting. If you land here, 
put your token in the Just Visiting section.  
Free Parking 
Nothing happens. This is the perfect time to 
take a break from your Jedi training. There’s 
a restaurant at the wharf that serves delicious 
space waffles.
THE FORCE AND  
THE DARK SIDE  
Take the top card from the matching deck. If the 
card says to keep it until you’re ready to use it, 
do so. Otherwise, read it aloud, and immediately 
do what it says. Return the card to the bottom of 
the appropriate deck when done.
Go to Jail  
Move your token to the In Jail space immediately!

How do I get out of Jail?
You have 3 options:
1.  Pay 10 at the start of your next turn, then roll and move  
as normal. 
2.  Use a Get Out of Jail Free card at the start of your next turn if 
you have one (or buy one from another player). Put the card at the 
bottom of the appropriate deck, then roll and move.
3.  Roll a 6 on your next turn. If you do, you’re free! Use the roll to 
move, and that’s the end of your turn.  
You can use up to three turns to try for a 6. If you don’t roll a 6  
by your third turn in Jail, pay 10, and use your last roll to move.

next one. Follow the rules of the space where

THE BOARD SPACES
PROPERTIES 
There is one type of property: alliances with heroic figures, 
which come in color sets.
UNFORMED ALLIANCES  
When you land on a heroic figure that no player has formed an alliance 
with, you must form an alliance with them! Pay the price on the board 
space, and take that heroic figure’s alliance card.
If you can’t afford to pay, try to raise credits by selling Jedi 
Starfighters back to the Bank, or by selling The Force cards and/or Get 
Out of Jail Free cards to other players for an agreed-upon price. 
If you still can’t pay, then you are bankrupt and out of the game! 
Return any of your The Force cards and/or Get Out of Jail Free cards to 
the bottom of the appropriate deck.
