OBJECT
Build your galactic empire by traveling around the board 
and purchasing iconic Star Wars properties along the 
way. Earn money by charging players rent to visit places 
you own. 
HOW TO WIN
The game ends when all the properties are purchased. 
The player with the most property markers on the board 
at the end of the game wins! If there is a tie, the player 
with the most money wins.
SET IT UP!
1. Open the gameboard tray and  
place it in reach of each player.
2. Choose your ship token and place  
it on GO.
3. Find the 16 cardboard property  
markers that match your ship and  
keep them in front of you.
4. Shuffle the Chance cards and place them 
face down on the Chance space in the tray. 
5. Pick one player to be the Banker.

AUCTIONS
If you land on an unowned property and you don’t want to buy it, the Banker must auction it. 
1. The Banker starts the auction by offering the space to everyone for A10.
2. Anyone can increase the bid by as little as A1 (even the Banker and the player who  
originally landed on the space).
3. The highest bidder wins the auction, pays the Bank, and places their property marker  
in the slot by the property’s edge.
WHAT IF NO ONE WANTS IT?
If no one would like to bid for the property, it remains unowned.

GAME GUIDE
 Each player should receive a total of a1,500.  
Keep the rest of the money to the side as the Bank.
HELP! I CAN’T PAY!
DO YOU OWE ANOTHER PLAYER?
• Try bargaining with the Get Out of Jail Free card, if you have it. 
• Sell them your properties to pay off the debt.
Properties can be sold at half their purchase value, or traded to the satisfaction of both players. 
If you still cannot pay, you must give all your properties to the player you owe. You are now bankrupt and out of the game.
DO YOU OWE THE BANK?
Sell your properties to the Bank to pay off your debt.
• Properties can be sold at half their purchase value.
• Any properties sold back to the Bank are now available to purchase as normal when a player lands on that space on 
the board. 
If you still cannot pay the Bank…
You must give any properties you have left to the Bank, which are then immediately auctioned among the other players. 
Return the Get Out of Jail Free card to the bottom of the Chance pile, if you have it. You are now bankrupt and out of  
the game.
The remaining players keep playing until all properties are purchased.
WIN!
1. Once all properties on the board have been purchased, the game ends.  
The player who owns the most properties is the winner.
2. In case of a tie, the player with the most money wins!

HOW DO I GET OUT OF JAIL?
You have 3 options:
1. Roll a double on any of your next three 
turns; if you succeed in doing this, you 
immediately move forward the number 
of spaces shown on your dice. Even 
though you rolled doubles, you do not 
get to take another turn.
2. Use the Get Out of Jail Free card at the  
start of your next turn if you have it.  
(You can also try buying or trading for 
it from another player.) Put the card at 
the bottom of the Chance pile, then roll 
and move. 
3. Pay a fine of A50 before your turn.  
You may then roll the dice and take  
your turn as usual.
If you do not roll doubles by your third 
turn, you must pay the A50 fine. You then 
get out of jail immediately and move 
forward the number of spaces shown on 
your dice.

CHANCE
• Take the top Chance card, read it out loud,  
and follow the instructions.
• Return used cards to the bottom of the pile.
PLAY!
Each player rolls one die. The highest roller will start the game.  
Play continues to the left. 
1. Roll both dice and move your ship token that number of spaces  
clockwise. Always move forward, never backward.
2. If you roll a double, move, then roll the dice again, and take another turn. 
Watch out! If you roll a double three times in the same turn, you must go to jail!
3. Where did you land? Check THE BOARD SPACES section of this guide.
4. Your turn is over. The player on your left goes next. 
Owning a color set: 
If you own all properties of the same color  
(for instance all three Endor properties),  
the rent for these properties is doubled!  
USE THE FORCE 
Landing on a “USE THE FORCE” space grants you a special power.  
When you land on the space:
1. Roll one die.
2. Match the number you rolled to one of the six different actions in the middle section  
of the gameboard.
3. Follow the instructions depending on what you rolled: 
1. Mind Trick: Swap one of your properties with the property of a player of your choice.
2. Force Stealth: Move to any unowned space and purchase this space for free. Do not  
collect A200 for passing GO.
3. Force Levitate: Swap the location of your ship with any other ship on the board.  
Do not collect A200 for passing GO. The turn ends as soon as the positions are swapped.
4. Force Pull: Move any player to one of your owned properties and collect rent.
5. Mind Trick: Steal A50 from each player.
6. Force Dash: Move directly to GO and collect A200.
Don’t want to buy the property  
or can’t afford it? 
Then the Banker must auction it. Bids start at A10.  
All players may bid. If no one would like to bid for  
the property, it remains unowned. See the AUCTIONS 
section on the back page for more information.
