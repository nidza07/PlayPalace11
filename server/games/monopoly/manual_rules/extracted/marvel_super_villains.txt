Adding Stolen Tech
As soon as you get a color set, you can start 
buying Stolen Tech (you don’t have to wait  
for your turn). 
Pay the Bank the cost on the contract card,  
and place a Stolen Tech on that contract.
You must upgrade evenly. You cannot have  
a second Stolen Tech on a contract until you  
have one on each contract in the set. You can 
have 4 Stolen Tech on a contract. 
IF YOU CAN’T PAY…IF YOU CAN’T PAY…
1  Try to raise Cosmic  
Energy Units.
If you owe payment, a Reshape the Universe or 
Chance card fee, or you land on an unrecruited 
contract and can’t pay, try to raise Cosmic Energy 
Units by selling back upgrades or selling Reshape  
the Universe or Get Out of Jail Free cards.  
No stealing!
Selling Upgrades
Sell Bio Upgrades back to the Bank for their cost,  
and immediately exchange them for 4 Stolen Tech.
Sell Stolen Tech back to the Bank for their cost. 
Stolen Tech must be sold evenly across the color set.
Still owe Cosmic Energy Units?
If you still owe a player payment and don’t have  
any Stolen Tech or Bio Upgrades to sell, you must 
transfer one of your contracts to that player to settle 
your debt.
If you still owe the Bank, return one contract to  
the Bank to settle your debt.
  
2  If you don’t have any contracts 
left, you are bankrupt and out  
of the game!
Do you owe another player?
Give them any Reshape the Universe and/or  
Get Out of Jail Free cards you have.
Do you owe the Bank?
Return any Reshape the Universe and/or Get Out 
of Jail Free cards you have to the bottom of the 
appropriate deck.
The remaining players keep playing until there  
is only one person left in the game. That player  
is the winner—the universe’s most diabolical  
Super Villain!

Adding Bio Upgrades
Once you have 4 Stolen Tech on all contracts  
in a color set, you can pay for Bio Upgrades.
Pay the Bio Upgrade cost on the contract card, 
return all 4 Stolen Tech to the Bank, and put  
a Bio Upgrade on the contract.
You can only have 1 Bio Upgrade per contract. 
You can’t add any more Stolen Tech.
Not enough upgrades?
If multiple players want to buy the last Stolen 
Tech or Bio Upgrade, the Banker must auction 
it. Bids start at 1 Cosmic Energy Unit and 
anyone can increase the bid by as little as  
1 Cosmic Energy Unit. You don’t need to  
follow turn order. Payment goes to the Bank.
No upgrades left?
You can’t buy any until someone sells  
theirs back.

How do I get out of Jail?
You have 3 options:
1. Pay 1 Cosmic Energy Unit at the start of your 
next turn, then roll and move as normal.
2. Use a Get Out of Jail Free card at the start of 
your next turn if you have one (or buy one from 
another player). Put the card at the bottom of  
the appropriate deck, then roll and move.  
3. Roll 6 on your next turn. If you  
do, you’re free! Use the roll to  
move, and that’s the end of your 
turn. You can use up to three 
turns to try for a 6. If you don’t 
roll a 6 by your third turn  
in Jail, pay 1 Cosmic  
Energy Unit, and  
use your last roll  
to move.

PROPERTIES
There are two types of properties: Super Villain contracts, which come in color  
sets, and army contracts, which are the Chitauri and Skrull Army.
Unrecruited Contracts
When you land on an unrecruited Super Villain, you must recruit them!  
Pay the price on the board space, and take that Super Villain’s contract card. 
If you can’t afford to pay, try to raise Cosmic Energy Units by selling  
back Stolen Tech, Bio Upgrades, or Reshape the Universe and/or  
Get Out of Jail Free cards. 
If you still can’t pay, then you are bankrupt and out of the game! 
Return any of your Reshape the Universe and/or Get Out of 
Jail Free cards to the bottom of the appropriate deck.
Collect color sets!
Recruited Contracts
When you land on a contract that another player has recruited, that player must ask you for payment.  
If they do, you must pay. If they don’t ask before the next player rolls the die, you don’t have to pay!
