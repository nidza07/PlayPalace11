Building Forts
As soon as you get a color set,  
you can start building Forts around 
Hawkins (you don’t have to wait  
for your turn).  
Pay the Bank the cost price on the Title Deed card 
and place a Fort on the Location.
Build evenly. You cannot build a second Fort on a 
Location until you’ve built one on each Location in 
the set. You can only have 4 Forts on a Location. 
Building Hideouts
Once you have 4 Forts on all 
Locations in a complete color set, 
you can pay to combine the Forts 
into a Hideout.
Pay the Hideout cost price on the Title Deed card, 
return all 4 Forts to the Bank, and put a Hideout on 
the Location.
You can only have 1 Hideout per Location.  
You can’t add any more Forts.
You cannot build on a Location if any Location  
in its color set is mortgaged. 
Not enough buildings?
If multiple players want the last Fort or Hideout, the 
Banker must auction it. Bids start at A10.
No buildings left?
You can’t buy any until someone sells theirs back.
Auctions
If you land on a Location, Vehicle, or Utility and 
don’t want to search it or buy it, the Banker must 
auction it.
1.  The Banker starts the auction by offering the 
space to everyone for A10.
2.  Anyone can increase the bid by as little as A1 
(even the Banker and the player who originally 
landed on the space).
3.  The highest bidder wins the auction, pays the 
Bank, and takes the Title Deed card.
What if no one wants it?
That’s fine. No one pays anything.
Deals & Trades
You can buy, sell, or swap property with other 
players at any time.
You must sell all buildings on a color set to the  
Bank before you can sell or trade a Location.  
You cannot sell (or trade) buildings to another player.
Property can be traded for cash, other property, 
and/or Get Out of Jail Free cards. The amount is 
decided by the players making the deal.
Mortgaged property can be traded at any  
agreed price.
The new owner must immediately either:
Repay the mortgage  (pay the Bank the 
unmortgage cost).
Or keep the mortgage (just pay the Bank  
10% of the mortgage value now).
HELP! I CAN’T PAY!BUILDINGS
If you’re still in debt, you’re bankrupt!  
You are now trapped in the Upside Down 
and out of the game!
Do you owe another player?
Give them any Get Out of Jail Free cards  
and all your mortgaged properties.
The new owner must immediately either:
Repay the mortgage  (pay the Bank the 
unmortgage cost).
Or keep the mortgage (just pay the Bank 
10% of the mortgage value now).
 
Do you owe the Bank?
Return all your properties to the Bank.  
Any mortgages are canceled.
All your properties must immediately be put 
up for auction. 
Return any Get Out of Jail Free cards to the 
bottom of the pile.
 The remaining players keep playing until 
there is only one person left who isn’t 
trapped in the Upside Down. This person 
has rescued Will and is the winner!
Try to raise money.
If you owe money and can’t pay, try to  
raise money by selling buildings and/or  
mortgaging properties. 
Selling Buildings
   Sell Hideouts to the Bank for half the cost 
price and exchange them immediately for  
4 Forts. Sell Forts  to the Bank for half the 
cost price. Forts must be sold evenly across 
the color set. 
Mortgaging Property
To mortgage a property, you must first sell all 
buildings in its color set to the Bank at half 
their cost price. 
To mortgage, turn the Title Deed card face 
down and collect the mortgage value on the 
back from the Bank. 
To repay a mortgage, pay the unmortgage 
cost to the Bank (mortgage value +10%), then 
turn the card face up.
Rent cannot be collected on properties that 
are mortgaged. However, the increased rent 
level can be collected on the unmortgaged 
Locations in a color set.
The increased rent on unmortgaged Vehicles 
and Utilities may be collected in the same way.

NEVER LOAN MONEY TO OTHER PLAYERS,

HOW TO WIN
Move around the board, searching as many Locations 
and buying as many properties as you can. The more 
you search, the more likely you’ll stay in the game. If 
you’re the last player left when all other players have 
gone bankrupt and gotten trapped in the Upside 
Down, you have found Will, and you are the winner!
