TOP TIPS  
To keep your game short and sweet, don’t use house rules!
Always auction when someone doesn’t want to take control of the property they’ve landed on.
Never loan Energon to other players or make deals not to charge each other energy fees. 
Never put Energon in the center of the board; you don’t get extra Energon for landing on Free Parking!

Obtaining the 
Golden Disk
You’ll place the Golden Disk

at the start of the game.
If you ever pass or land on

Wielding the Golden Disk
When you have the Golden Disk,  
you’ll move it around the board with  
your token.
If you’re in robot mode, you can:
 • Charge 4 more Energon (in energy  
  fees) when another player lands

 • Collect 4 more Energon when

 • Collect 4 more Energon when  
  you draw a card that tells you  
  to collect.
If you’re in alt mode, you may not  
wield the Golden Disk’s special powers.
Surrendering the Golden Disk
In robot mode or alt mode, you  
must immediately surrender the  
Golden Disk when:
 • Another player passes or lands  
  on your space. They take the  
  Golden Disk and its card.
 • You are sent to Jail or draw a  
  card that tells you to surrender  
  the Disk. Place the Golden Disk  
  on the Free Parking space.

STASIS PODS 
As soon as you get a color set, you  
can start adding Stasis Pods. You don’t  
have to wait for your turn—but you do  
have to be in robot mode.
To add a Stasis Pod, pay the Bank the cost on the  
Title Deed card, and place a Stasis Pod on the location. 
You can only have 1 Stasis Pod per location. You cannot 
add a Stasis Pod to a location if either location in its color 
set is drained of power. 
Not enough Stasis Pods?
If multiple players want to buy the last Stasis 
Pod, the Banker must auction it. Bids start at  
1 Energon and anyone can increase the bid by as 
little as 1 Energon. You don’t need to follow turn 
order. Payment goes to the Bank. 
No Stasis Pods left? 
You can’t buy any until someone sells theirs back.

WHAT’S DIFFERENT ABOUT 
MONOPOLY: 
TRANSFORMERS  
BEAST WARS™?
Two tokens per player!
You play as either a Maximal or a 
Predacon, and use two interchangeable 
character tokens throughout the game 
(one for robot mode, one for alt mode). 
Each mode has its own strengths and 
weaknesses (see REMEMBER, BOTS! on 
back page), and you can start the game in 
whichever mode you wish. Place your 
starting token on the GO space and the 
other token in front of you. Then, 
whenever you land on a Maximize/
Terrorize space, you’ll switch the two 
tokens around!
Wield the Golden Disk!
The Golden Disk starts each game 
on the Free Parking space, and 
the first player to pass or land on 
that space takes the Golden Disk. 
For the remainder of the game, 
whenever any player passes or 
lands on the Golden Disk’s space (whether it is with another 
player’s token or not), they immediately take the Golden Disk!
     The Golden Disk allows you to wield special powers, but 
only while you’re in robot mode. For example, you might be 
able to charge more energy fees (rent) and collect more 
Energon when you pass or land on the GO space or draw 
certain cards.
Beware Energon radiation!
Whenever you’re in robot mode, you’re at risk of Energon 
radiation as you travel to energy-rich locations on the primitive 
planet where you now find yourself trapped. If you are in robot 
mode when you land on a location that someone else controls 
(owns), you pay a higher energy fee than you would in alt 
mode. So being in alt mode has its advantages!
PLAY!
How to win
Travel around a primitive planet as a heroic 
Maximal or a villainous Predacon! Race to 
take control of key locations and powerful 
spaceships, obtain Stasis Pods, and wield  
the Golden Disk! If you’re the last player  
with Energon when all other players have 
depleted theirs, you win!
 
Who goes first?
Each player rolls the die.
The highest roller starts, and play moves  
to the left.
On your turn
1. Roll the die.
2. Move your token (and the Golden Disk  
 if you have it) clockwise that number  
 of spaces.
 Note: If the Golden Disk is on your  
 starting space, and you do not have it  
 already, leave it there.
3. Where did you land? Carry out the  
 rules of that board space.
 See THE BOARD SPACES.
If you pass or land  
on the Golden Disk,  
take it immediately!  
You will move it around 
the board with your  
token. Place the Golden 
Disk card in front of you. 
If you already have the  
Golden Disk and must go to Jail, 
place it on the Free Parking space.
See THE GOLDEN DISK.
4. Your turn ends. Pass the die to your left. 
THE BOARD SPACES
PROPERTIES
There are two types of properties: 
locations, which come in color sets, 
and spaceships. 
Uncontrolled Properties
When you land on an uncontrolled property,  
you must take control of it or auction it. 
Want to take control of it? 
Pay the price on the board space, and take the Title Deed  
card from the Bank. 
Don’t want to take control of it? Auction it!
The Banker must auction it. Bidding starts at 1 Energon, and anyone can 
increase the bid by as little as 1 Energon. You don’t need to follow turn order, 
and the Banker ends the auction when no player is willing to increase the bid.  
The highest bidder pays the Bank. If no one wants to bid on the property,  
that’s fine. No one pays anything, and the Title Deed stays with the Bank. 
Collect color sets! 
When you control each location in a color set: 
 • You can charge more energy fees for those locations! 
 • You may add Stasis Pods (but only while you are in robot mode) 
  and charge even more energy fees! See STASIS PODS.
Controlled Properties
When you land on a property that someone else controls, that player must ask you for energy fees.  
If they do, you must pay. If they don’t ask before the next player rolls the die, you don’t have to pay!

How do I get out of Jail?
You have 3 options:
1. Pay 1 Energon at the start of your next turn, 
 then roll and move as normal.
2. Use a Get Out of Jail Free card at the start of  
 your next turn if you have one (or buy one from  
 another player). Put the card at the bottom of the  
 appropriate deck, then roll and move. 
3. Roll a 6 on your next turn. If you do, you’re free!  
 Use the roll to move, and that’s the end of your turn. 
 You can use up to 3 turns to try for a 6. If you  
 don’t roll a 6 by your third turn in Jail, pay 
 1 Energon, and use your last roll to move.
 
 If the Golden Disk is on Just Visiting when you 
leave Jail, you may not take it.
