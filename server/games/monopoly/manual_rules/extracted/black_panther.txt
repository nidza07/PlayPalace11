Building Strongholds
Once you have 4 Villages for all Alliances in a color set, you 
can pay to upgrade to a Stronghold.
Pay the Stronghold cost on the Alliance card, return all 
4 Villages to the Bank, and place a Stronghold for the 
Alliance. You can only have 1 Stronghold per Alliance. You 
can’t add any more Villages.
You cannot build on an Alliance if any other Alliances in its 
color set are mortgaged. 
Not enough buildings?
If multiple players want to buy the last Village or Stronghold, the Banker 
must auction it. Bids start at 10 and anyone can increase the bid by as 
little as 1. You don’t need to follow turn order. Payment goes to the Bank.
No buildings left? 
You can’t buy any until someone  sells theirs back.

Building Villages
As soon as you get a color set, you can start buying 
Villages (you don’t have to wait for your turn). 
Pay the Bank the cost on the Alliance card, and place 
a Village for that Alliance.
You must build evenly. You cannot build a second 
Village for an Alliance until you’ve built one for each 
Alliance in the set. You can only have 4 Villages per 
Alliance.
Try to raise money.

TRADES
You can buy, sell, or swap 
Alliances, Transports, and 
Utilities with other players at 
any time.  
You must sell all buildings  
on a color set to the Bank 
before you can sell or trade  
an Alliance. You cannot sell 
or trade buildings to another 
player.
Alliances can be traded for 
cash, other Alliances, and/
or Get Out of Jail Free cards. 
The amount is decided by the 
players making the deal.
Mortgaged Alliances can be 
traded at any agreed-upon price.
The new owner must 
immediately: 
Repay the mortgage  (pay the 
Bank the unmortgage cost).
Or keep the mortgage  
(just pay the Bank 10% of  
the mortgage value now). Rent cannot be collected on Alliances that 
are mortgaged. However, the increased rent 
level can be collected on the unmortgaged 
Alliances in a color set.
The increased rent on unmortgaged 
Transports and Utilities may be collected in 
the same way.
Selling Buildings
Sell Strongholds to the Bank for half the price, and 
exchange them immediately for 4 Villages.
 
Sell Villages to the Bank for half the cost price. 
Villages must be sold evenly across the color set.
Mortgaging Alliances
To mortgage an Alliance, you must first sell all 
buildings in its color set to the Bank at half their 
cost price. 
To mortgage, turn the Title Deed card facedown, 
and collect the mortgage value on the back from 
the Bank. 
To repay a mortgage, pay the unmortgage cost 
to the Bank (mortgage value +10%); then, turn 
the card faceup.

How do I get out of Jail?
You have 3 options:
 1   Pay 50 at the start of your next turn; then, roll and move as normal.
 2    Use a Get Out of Jail Free card at the start of your next turn if you have one (or 
buy one from another player). Put the card at the bottom of the appropriate deck; 
then, roll and move. 
 3   Roll a double on your next turn. If you do, you’re free! Use the roll to move, and 
that’s the end of your turn. 
You can use up to 3 turns to try for a double. If you don’t roll a double by your third 
turn In Jail, pay 50, and use your last roll to move.
